Quick & Easy to Use Fencing Training Framework

FencingDynamics is a Python-based framework designed to make fencing practice simple and interactive. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fencer, you can train with real-time motion tracking and AI-powered simulations. Say goodbye to traditional practice methods and embrace a smarter way to hone your skills.

Modern design with Essential Features

Our intuitive interface combines the elegance of fencing with cutting-edge technology. It tracks your movements, analyzes your strikes, and increases your score whenever you successfully hit your opponent. This seamless experience provides actionable feedback to help you refine your technique and master the art of fencing

Crafted for Fencers and Coaches

Built for individual fencers and trainers alike, FencingDynamics enables you to simulate realistic drills, adapt to various scenarios, and prepare for your next match with precision. It's your ultimate tool for mastering the art of fencing.
